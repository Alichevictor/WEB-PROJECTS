We actively render the following
services as stated below

Antenatal(prenatal) Care Services:

This is a type of preventive health care services
that is rendered to pregnant women throughout
their period of pregnancy .It is aimed at having a healthy mother and baby
at the end of care.
It includes risk identification card screening,
prevention and management of pregnancy related infections,
health education and promotion.

Out Patient Services:

This is a service provided for patient
that do not need an overnight stay in the hospital. . It does not require hospitalization.

In patient Services:

This is a form of service provided for a patient
that is formally admitted for treatment
.The patient stays for a minimum of one night in the hospital.

General Medical Practice:

A good medical practice describes what it means to be a good Doctor.
. This includes medical consultation and treatment
in the field of general medicine carried out by general practitioners.

Medical counselling services:

This service is provided to work with clients
experiencing a wide range of emotional and psychological
difficulties. .This is to help them bring about effective change and/or enhance their well-being.
